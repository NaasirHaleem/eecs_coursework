The Ugly Duckling (Working Link)
A young duckling is ostracized by his family. They, as well as the other animals on the farm, make fun of his looks. That is until he comes to a startling revelation and realizes that appearances truly are all that matters in the end.
Little Red Riding Hood
A young girl wants to have lunch with her grandmother. However, there is a terrible wolf lurking in the forest. Read on in this riveting tale of eat or be eaten.
Jack and The Beanstalk
Jack is a young boy living in a poor village. When given the chance to trade his family's livestock for some magical beans, he wisely jumps at the chance. What happens next is beyond his, or anyone's in this specific situation, wildest dreams.
The Tortoise and the Hare
A Tortoise has had enough of the antics of an arrogant Hare. The Tortoise shockingly decides to challenge the Hare to a race. Read on and learn about the intellectually questionable tale of not playing to one's strengths and somehow still persevering.
Goldilocks and the Three Bears
A young thief breaks into the home of a peaceful family of bears. Will the family of bears survive their encounter with this maniacal criminal? Will justice prevail?
The Legend of Sleepy Hollow
In this tale of witchcraft, a mysterious horseman terrorizes a small town. A lovelorn man wanders into this town, unaware of the danger. What will become of him?
