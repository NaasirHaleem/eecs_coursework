Biography

Hans Christian Anderson was born in Odense, Denmark in 1805. Although he faced a tough educational career, citing cases of abuse and ridicule by the faculty of schools that he attended, he went on to become one of the most prolific writers of the time. He wrote over 3,380 fairy tales. These works have become a mainstay of Western literature.
